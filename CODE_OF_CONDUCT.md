# Code of Conduct

Funke is a small project that intends to stay pleasant. Participation in this
repository — issues, discussions, pull requests, plugin listings — comes with a few
expectations.

## Expectations

- **Be respectful.** Critique code and ideas, never people. No insults, harassment,
  discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- **Be constructive.** "This is broken" plus a reproduction beats a rant; a suggestion
  beats a demand. Assume good faith — most disagreements are misunderstandings.
- **Stay on topic.** This is a space for working on a launcher, not for spam,
  self-promotion, or off-topic controversy.
- **Respect maintainer time.** Funke is maintained in free time; issues and reviews
  may take a while. Bumping a thread daily doesn't make it faster.
- **Mind security.** Never post other people's private data, and report
  vulnerabilities privately per [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) rather than in public
  issues.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, lock, or remove content and restrict or ban participants who
violate these expectations — escalating consequences for repeated or severe behavior,
without prior warning for egregious cases (harassment, doxxing, malicious code).

To report a violation, email **bigbenwashere@gmail.com**. Reports are handled
confidentially.

*(This is an intentionally short, plain-language code of conduct; it may be replaced
with the Contributor Covenant once the project's licensing/attribution stance is
settled.)*
